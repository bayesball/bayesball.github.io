Jim Albert

Bio

Jim Albert is Emeritus Professor, Department of Mathematics and Statistics, Bowling Green State University. He has interests in Bayesian modeling, statistics education and the application of statistical thinking in sports.

Books/Notes/Papers

  • Current Baseball Research
  • A Course in Exploratory Data Analysis (based on Tukey's EDA book)
  • Probability and Bayesian Modeling (with Monika Hu)
    Bookdown Version
  • Visualizing Baseball
  • Analyzing Baseball with R, Third Edition (with Max Marchi and Ben Baumer)
  • R by Example (with Maria Rizzo)
  • Bayesian Computation with R
  • Curve Ball (with Jay Bennett)
  • Teaching Statistics Using Baseball
  • Ordinal Data Modeling (with Val Johnson)
  • Workshop Statistics: Discovery with Data, A Bayesian Approach (with Allan Rossman)
  • Data Analysis and Probability for Teachers
  • Bayesian Computation Using Minitab
