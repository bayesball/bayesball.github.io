Data Analysis and Probability for Teachers by Jim Albert 

A data analysis and probability textbook for prospective and in -service Middle School 
and High School teachers. The National Council of Teachers in Mathematics (NCTM) 
has established standards in data analysis and probability to be taught to all students 
from grades K-12.  This text is designed to teach the prospective teachers basic concepts 
in probability and statistics so they have the background to teach the content listed in 
the NCTM standards.
